Ebook Design

  1. Home

Looking for a captivating ebook that generates leads?

Since we've been creating eBooks for more than three years, we've aided over a thousand entrepreneurs, coaches, and professionals, and we've found that these digital assets never stop producing results. Whether you want to grow your email list, increase brand recognition, or establish trust, you need this.

We create the eBook cover to catch the reader's eye and pique their interest in downloading it. Next, We create the interior pages to appeal to the reader and keep them reading until they reach the CTA page.

In addition to delivering a PDF and providing the InDesign source file, We also produce a 3D mockup for your marketing efforts.

While designing or redesigning your PDF, ebook, workbook, or lead magnet, requirements must be provided, including references, logos, websites, and color schemes.

Note:

Content creation is not covered. No content on the theme of gambling, religion, or sexual orientation will be used. Final File will be delivered as a PDF rather than a Word document or Canva source.
